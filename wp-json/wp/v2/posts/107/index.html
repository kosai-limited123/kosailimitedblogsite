What is Insertion sort?<\/h3>\n\n\n\n
<\/div>\n\n\n\n

Insertion sort is a kind of simple sorting algorithm that creates a final arranged or sorted list taking one item at a time.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

<\/div>\n\n\n\n

Example<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

Consider you have 5 cards out of a deck of cards in your hand . And they are sorted in the ascending order of their number. If I give you one card and ask you to  insert the card in your hand what will you do? You will find the lower or the higher number of the card and insert it. This is called insertion sort.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
