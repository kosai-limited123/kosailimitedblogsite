What is Selection Sort?<\/h3>\n\n\n\n
<\/div>\n\n\n\n

There are numerous sorting methods and selection sort is one of them .Which uses two part: one is sorted and another is unsorted.  It  repeatedly finds the minimum element from unsorted part and putting it at  the sorted part. Its same as insertion sort but difference is here the sorted part is empty.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
